Subject: Application for Leave

Dear [Manager's Name],

I am writing to inform you that I will be unable to attend work on [date(s)] due to personal reasons. I am requesting [number of days] off for my leave.

I apologize for any inconvenience my absence may cause and I assure you that I will make arrangements to ensure that my responsibilities are covered during my absence. I will check my email periodically and will be available to respond to any urgent matters.

I would also like to assure you that I will return to work as soon as my leave is over and will be ready to resume my duties with full energy and dedication.

Thank you for considering my request.

Sincerely,
Lakshay

Note:

* Be sure to include the specific dates of your leave and the number of days you will be absent.
* If possible, give advance notice of your leave.
* If you are requesting leave for a specific reason, such as a medical appointment or a family emergency, it may be helpful to include that information in your application.
* Make sure to assure that your responsibilities are covered during your absence and you will check your email periodically and will be available to respond to any urgent matters.
* Be polite and professional in your tone, and express your gratitude for the consideration of your request.
